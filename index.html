USSD Technology

USSD is a communication protocol that uses codes made up of the characters available on a mobile phone. These codes allow communication between a mobile device to a network or server.

With USSD you don't need internet access or a smartphone to access the Ethereum Dapp ecosystem. This means that the underprivellaged can now access Ethereum without major boundaries of entry.

Instant Messaging

Ethereum Dapp access with popular instant messaging services allows for a wider market to be reached. This allows access to popular dapps just from messaging apps which reduces data usage.

    - WhatsApp
    - Telegram
    - Viber
    - Facebook Messager

Decentralised Finance

Decentralised Finance (DeFi) allows anyone from anywhere to access financial technology with the benefits of blockchain technology.

Now with MicroDapp you can access DeFi with or without a smartphone or internet access.

This means the unbanked will now have access to financial tools to help them save and invest in their future. Like DAI as a stable currency over the unstable currency in countries like Venezuela and Zimbabwe. Compound finance for saving and earning interest and InstaDapp for borrowing DAI.

Decentralised Organisations

Powerful decentralised autonomous organisations that allow small communities to group resources and distribute them equally among the community with democratically voted rules.

This allows for a cheaper and more efficient organisation with accountability and transparency. This is needed at the ground level where resources are scarce.

Decentralised Work

With MicroDapp you can access microwork through the power of bounties. Work is logged and funds are stored in smart contracts until tasks are completed. There are no intermeddiaries and centralised control over the tasks.
